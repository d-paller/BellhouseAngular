About Us

We are in the heart of Downtown Shelbyville in a home that was fully renovated from 2007-2009 by Bobby and Sue Andriot. If you visit "Our Story" page, you can read about the progress that this house has been through in the past century.

We are striving to be fine dining, but still delight in the "down home hospitality" that makes you feel welcome.

There is no particular dress code; We will take you as you are. Reservations are not necessary, but they are accepted and appreciated.

Good Service, Good Food, and a Beautiful Environment...you can't go wrong! We look forward to serving you!
